BASE

Biometric Atomic Structure Encryption

What is it?

Using the technology of mass spectrometry, a chemical key can be used to authenticate a lock. A keycard with a specific compound it put inside a slot, and if your compound matches the key and you pass the biometric encryption with a fingerprint scan, the lock unlocks.


How does it work?

Mass Spectrometry can be used to identify the chemical you're looking at with ions!


How is it secure?

There are infinite amounts of combinations of chemicals. This creates a very secure lock!


Applications

Bank vaults, data centers, and more
